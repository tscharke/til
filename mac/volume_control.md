# Volume control
## Keyboard
* (Fn) + Volume up
* (Fn) + Volume down

### Finer volume control
* (Fn) + Shift + ^ + Volume up
* (Fn) + Shift + ^ + Volume down

## With Touchbar
### Finer volume control
* (Fn) + Shift + ⌥ + Volume up
* (Fn) + Shift + ⌥ + Volume down

*Legend*:
* **Fn** *Function key*
* **^**  *Control key*
* **⌥** *Option key*
* **()** *Optional*
